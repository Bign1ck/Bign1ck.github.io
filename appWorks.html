Touch Care App

This Elderly Care App is made using Adobe XD

- The purpose of this app is to give extra support to the elderly when their guardians are not around. Furthermore, there are some mini activities that they can do if they are bored.

- Design Concept: The colour scheme mainly consists of light blue as research shows that is a favourite colour of elderly due to it's calming properties. Typography used is Montserrat as it is clean and easy on the eyes.

Features include:

1. Activity feature such as "How to Yoga"

2. A daily reminder systems for taking meds, drinking water, etc

3. Language changer

4. Customer support
